# Treasure Hunt## ChallengeYar! Where there be treasure there be pirates!  Your mission should you choose to accept it will be to go on a hackers treasure hunt for pirate treasure.  Grab a jacket, pencil and paper, a WiFi device, and a [map](http://www.fpm.iastate.edu/maps/).To get started, Call: `1 (877) 844-1072` (toll free).### Notes
- This challenge involves making calls from [free public phones on campus](http://www.it.iastate.edu/howtos/dial).  From campus phones you must dial `8` first before dialing the toll free number.  So on campus you will dial `8-1-877-844-1072`.- All teams that complete this challenge will receive points [30 points].  This is not a race.  Please be respectful on campus of other students not engaged in this competition.- Note partial credit may be possible.  Submit a log of your activities if you are unable to complete this challenge.
